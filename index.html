Denver Open Source Users Group

Matthew McCullough, Mike Barker, Tim Berglund, Dan Hillenbrand, Mark Nelson, and Jordan McCullough welcome you to the Denver Open Source Users Group site. Shortened to the acronym DOSUG by our members, this group was founded in 2005 to help developers share information about Open Source projects in use at our day jobs.

Submissions

We always welcome speaker submissions on anything open source. Just drop the board an email describing your topic, the audience requirements, your speaking experience, and ability to travel to Denver if you have an OSS topic you are passionate about.

Mailing List

DOSUG uses a Meetup.com based member list for meeting notifications and member communication. In the spirit of education purity, we never sell, rent or disclose any aspect of member data, and we only send email regarding DOSUG events.
